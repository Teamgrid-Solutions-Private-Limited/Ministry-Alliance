Our Beliefs & Values

Discover how our faith shapes our work.

Our Guiding Foundation

We are an evangelical Christian organization that stands on an unwavering commitment to biblical authority. We affirm core Christian beliefs that unite and direct all our work. Agreement with our Statement of Belief ensures every ministry we support operates from the same spiritual foundation. This clarity keeps our mission focused and faithful to God’s Word.

Our History: A Legacy of
Service for 30+ Years

What began in 1991 as a small ministry support effort has become a global network of leaders united by one purpose. For more than three decades, Ministry Alliance has refined its approach to make ministry simpler, stronger, and more sustainable. Each year has deepened our commitment to serving those who serve others. Our story is one of growth, faith, and unwavering purpose.
