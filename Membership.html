Get Instant Non-Profit Status

The Member Ministry Program removes the barriers that delay ministry work. Instead of navigating forms and filings, you can immediately begin serving your community. We handle the legal structure so you can put your energy into outreach, discipleship, and impact. It's ministry made practical and sustainable from day one.

The Member Ministry Program Requirements.

1

Application Requirements

  • Submit a Member Ministry Application
  • Pay a $375 one-time application fee
  • Subscribe to the Ministry Alliance Statement of Belief
  • Live a life that honors God, is above reproach, and is worthy of your calling.
2

Full Membership Requirements

After your application is accepted, you must complete these steps:

  • Raise an initial $1,000 in donations.
  • Confirm you have read the "Member Ministry Handbook".
3

Ongoing Requirements

To maintain good standing, a member must:

  • Operate your ministry under the authority and accountability of the Ministry Alliance Board of Directors.
  • Abide by policies implemented and carried out by its leadership.
  • Raise a minimum of $10,000 per year.
  • Participate in an annual audit.
