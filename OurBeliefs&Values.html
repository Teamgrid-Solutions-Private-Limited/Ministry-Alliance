Our Beliefs & Values

Discover how our faith shapes our work.

Our Guiding Foundation

We are an evangelical Christian organization that stands on an unwavering commitment to biblical authority. We affirm core Christian beliefs that unite and direct all our work. Agreement with our Statement of Belief ensures every ministry we support operates from the same spiritual foundation. This clarity keeps our mission focused and faithful to God’s Word.

Our Statement of Belief

What we believe determines how we lead, serve, and support every ministry.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit's present ministry enables Christians to live a godly life.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

Salvation

We believe salvation is accomplished by grace alone through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

The Resurrection

We believe in the future resurrection of both the saved and the lost.

The Church

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in Jesus Christ.

Our Core Values

Our values are the living expression of the faith that drives our work.

The Great Commission

We are devoted to advancing the Great Commission of Jesus Christ by supporting ministries that make disciples of all nations.

Servant Leadership

We are called to serve and empower ministry leaders who shepherd others and extend the reach of the Gospel.

Kingdom Fellowship

We believe in the power of partnership within the Body of Christ, building relationships that strengthen and unite ministries in mission.

Godly Stewardship

We steward every resource entrusted to us with prayerful integrity, knowing that all we manage belongs to God and serves His purposes.

Whole-Life Ministry

We affirm that the Gospel meets both spiritual and physical needs, reflecting Christ's compassion for the whole person.

Sanctity of Life

We uphold the sacred worth of every human life, created in the image of God and deserving of care and protection from conception to natural death.

Covenant Marriage

We affirm God's design for marriage as a holy covenant between one man and one woman, reflecting Christ's relationship with His Church.

Religious Liberty

We stand for the God-given right to freely worship, proclaim, and live out our faith in every sphere of life.
